Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?
Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the billing accordingly.
What's included in the free trial?
The free trial includes full access to Pro features for 14 days, including unlimited AI strategies, advanced backtesting, and all data sources.
How do per-strategy notifications work?
Each strategy can have its own notification channel with custom alerts. You pay $1/month per strategy that has notifications enabled.
Are there any hidden fees?
No hidden fees! Our pricing is transparent. You only pay for the plan and any add-ons you choose. Data usage is unlimited on all paid plans.
Can I use my own data sources?
Yes! Business and Enterprise plans include API access to connect your own data sources and custom integrations.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise customers. All payments are secure and encrypted.
